Our Story

More Than Coffee —
A Way of Life

Velvet Brew was born from a simple belief: exceptional coffee deserves an exceptional setting. Founded in 2016 by barista duo Elena Mercer and James Holloway, our Brooklyn café has become the neighbourhood's heartbeat — a place where mornings begin, ideas bloom, and friendships deepen.

We source exclusively from small, sustainable farms in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala. Every roast is profiled in-house, and every cup is pulled with precision. It's obsessive, we know — and our regulars wouldn't have it any other way.

Why Velvet Brew

The Details Make the Difference

From the farm to your cup, every decision we make is guided by one question: does this make the experience better?

☕

Direct-Trade Beans

We work directly with 11 family farms across three continents, ensuring fair pay, sustainable farming, and coffee with provenance.

🔥

In-House Roasting

Our micro-roaster runs every Tuesday and Friday. Beans reach your cup within 72 hours of their perfect roast — freshness you can taste.

🌿

Sustainable Practices

Compostable packaging, zero food-waste partnerships, and solar-assisted energy — our commitment to the planet is as serious as our coffee.

🏆

Award-Winning Baristas

Two of our team members placed in the Northeast Regional Barista Championships. That level of skill shows up in every single drink we serve.
